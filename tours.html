Tours

Fixed wing flight

Fixed wing flights are $350 per hour for each passanger. One hour and two hour scenic flights are available as well as lake trips on our amphibian aircraft. Fully catered day trips are also available.

Hellicopter flight

Hellicopter flights are $450 per hour for each passanger. Mountain viewing trips (including Mt Cook) are available at sunset. Drop off and pick up for tramping trips is available. Photographic flights can be arranged and the doors can be removed by arrangement for professional assignments

Glider flights

Glider flights are $1200 per hour. A three hour flight is recomended
